Fan Weiqiu was a 62-year-old man who drove his SUV into people exercising at the Zhuhai Sports Center on November 11, 2024, killing 38 people and injuring 48 others Wikipedia (some sources report 35 deaths, with the death toll potentially rising afterward).
After the attack, Fan attempted suicide with a knife and was found unconscious in his car with self-inflicted wounds to his neck and chest. He was taken into custody and hospitalized. Wikipedia
According to police, Fan was motivated by anger over his recent divorce settlement and dissatisfaction with the division of financial assets. Wikipedia
